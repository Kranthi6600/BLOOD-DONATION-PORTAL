Who Can Give Blood...?

  • Individuals aged between 18 and 60 years
  • Weight must be at least 50 kilograms
  • Hemoglobin level should be at least 12.5 g/dL
  • Body temperature, blood pressure, and pulse must be normal
  • Should be free from infections or chronic diseases

Who Cannot Give Blood...?

  • If you've had a cold, fever, or flu in the last 2 weeks
  • If you've undergone surgery or major dental work recently
  • If you're currently pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Those with serious conditions like HIV, Hepatitis, or cancer
  • Recent tattoos or piercings within the last 6 months
